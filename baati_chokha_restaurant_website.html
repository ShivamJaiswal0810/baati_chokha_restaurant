Our Story

A Taste of Rajasthan
in the Heart of Deoria

Step through our doors and leave Deoria behind. Baati Chokha brings the ancient flavours of Rajasthan — slow-cooked in clay pots, seasoned with desert spices, served with the warmth of a thousand sunsets over the Thar.

Our restaurant is built on one belief: food is not just nourishment, it is memory. Every baati we bake, every chokha we smoke, carries the recipe of generations.

The Atmosphere

More than a meal —
an Experience

🏰
Rajasthani Décor
Mud walls, jharokha windows, traditional jaali work, and Rajputana artwork — every corner tells a story of the desert kingdom.
🪔
Clay Lamp Dining
Evenings lit by hundreds of diyas and lanterns. The warm flicker of light over a thali meal is a memory you carry home.
🎵
Folk Music
Live Rajasthani folk music on weekends — the soulful notes of the sarangi and khartal fill the air as you dine.
👘
Traditional Attire
Our staff dress in authentic Rajasthani attire — the vibrant bandhani and leheriya bring the colour of the state alive.
🍽️
Thali Service
Served on brass and copper thalis, in the traditional Rajasthani style — unlimited refills, true hospitality.
